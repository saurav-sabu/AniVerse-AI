**Joker** (2019)  
- **The Vibe:** Critics and fans praise Joaquin Phoenix’s haunting performance, calling it a “masterclass in vulnerability and chaos.” Reviews highlight its unflinching look at mental health, societal neglect, and the birth of a villain. Many note it’s a “superhero film for people who hate superhero films.”  
- **Why it matches:** As a standalone origin story that redefines the Joker mythos, it’s perfect for fans of psychological depth and antihero narratives.  
- **Watch:** Stream on HBO Max. Rent/Buy on Amazon, Apple TV, Google Play, and YouTube.

   - **Why it matches:** A darker, more grounded take on Gotham’s villains, with themes of chaos vs. order that echo *Joker*.  
   - **Watch:** Stream on Amazon Prime, HBO Max, and Disney+.

   - **Why it matches:** Like *Joker*, it explores how societal neglect can push someone to the brink. A must-watch for fans of gritty, character-driven dramas.  
   - **Watch:** Stream on Kanopy, Apple TV, and Amazon.
